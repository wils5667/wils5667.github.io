Dawson Knox is an American football player that inspires me. He currently plays for the buffalo bills as there tight end.

He is an amazing person on and off the field. On the field is a leader and after watching a lot of the bills game, you can notice it.

He carries himself with dignity. Off the field he will visit local buffalo schools and has a charity raising money for pediatric cancer.

Even after the loss of his brother, he still remain to be a good person on and off the field and gives his time and money to others.

He influences me to remember to never let life get you down and that you can always do something to help or support other people.

Dawson Knox also reminds me that the people we see on TV are just that, people, and we should always treat them with love and respect
